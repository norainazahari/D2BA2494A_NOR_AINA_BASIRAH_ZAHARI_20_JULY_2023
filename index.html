Welcome to ASRAMA PERKAYA BESUT

Loving, Sharing, Caring and Family.

Asrama PERKAYA Besut in Kampung Pondok Paku, Jabi, Besut began operating on January 1, 2007 with the recruitment of 34 students, namely 11 boys and 23 girls. They consist of Form 1, 2 and 3. They are placed to study at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Jabi, Besut which is 2km from the hostel. Recruitment of Year 5 and 6 primary school students on January 1, 2010, a total of 24 people (12 boys and 12 girls). They were placed to study at Sekolah Kebangsaan Bukit Tempurung, Besut. YB. Haji Ramlan bin Ali, Jabi assemblyman is the Chairman of the Dormitory Committee while Mr. Mohamed bin Awang is the Dormitory Supervisor.

Activities

2023 Eid Celebrations ASRAMA PERKAYA BESUT

The celebration of Hari Raya Aidilfitri 1444H/2023M organized by Asrama Perkaya Besut was filled with the laughter of orphans. These orphans celebrate Eid with full of joy. Fully dressed in baju melayu, songkok, baju kurung and a beautiful hijab. They enjoyed the sweets and food prepared with joy because they had successfully fasted for a whole month of Ramadhan. The memory of celebrating Ramadhan and Hari Raya together is the most beautiful memory for them.

Receiving Al-Quran Donations

On April 1, 2023, these lucky children received a donation of Al-Quran.

Invitation to attend a special evening lecture

Invitation to attend a special evening lecture on the eve of Ramadhan 1444H at Taman Ilmu Mosque, Besut. The lecture was delivered by Dr. Fauzi bin Yusoh, Senior Lecturer Unisza, Kuala Terengganu.
