About

Loving, Sharing, Caring and Family.

Asrama PERKAYA Besut in Kampung Pondok Paku, Jabi, Besut began operating on January 1, 2007 with the recruitment of 34 students, namely 11 boys and 23 girls. They consist of Form 1, 2 and 3. They are placed to study at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Jabi, Besut which is 2km from the hostel. Recruitment of Year 5 and 6 primary school students on January 1, 2010, a total of 24 people (12 boys and 12 girls). They were placed to study at Sekolah Kebangsaan Bukit Tempurung, Besut. YB. Haji Ramlan bin Ali, Jabi assemblyman is the Chairman of the Dormitory Committee while Mr. Mohamed bin Awang is the Dormitory Supervisor.
